We invite participants from all fields of robotics to submit extended abstracts related to the workshop topic, anywhere on the spectrum between model-based planning and model-free learning. Submissions should be up to 2-4 pages + references using the RSS template and should not be anonymized.

Submissions can include original research, late-breaking results, position papers, and literature reviews. Significant overlap with work submitted to other venues is acceptable, as long as it has not yet been published or presented.

Accepted submissions are required to provide pre-recorded 3-minute spotlight talks. All accepted abstracts and talks will be posted on this workshop website.
