Call for Contributions

We invite participants from all fields of robotics to submit extended abstracts related to the workshop topic, anywhere on the spectrum between model-based planning and model-free learning. Submissions should be up to 3 pages + references using the RSS template and should not be anonymized.

Submissions can include original research, late-breaking results, position papers, and literature reviews. Significant overlap with work submitted to other venues is acceptable, as long as it has not yet been published or presented.

Accepted submissions will be required to provide pre-recorded 3-minute spotlight talks and present at a dedicated poster session. All accepted abstracts will be posted on the workshop website.


Important dates

Submission deadline: June 20th

Notification of acceptance: July 1st

Camera-ready deadline: July 7th

Presentation video deadline: July 10th
